T.K International Travels & Tours (Pvt) Ltd has been immensely popular in the counseling & assistance of visas of all the countries and the magnitude of our achievements are well known across Pakistan. Documents Attestation

As part of the educational and professional requirements of certain educational institutions, embassies, and companies, Pakistani travellers are required to get their documents attested from relevant Pakistani offices and departments. In addition to booking and ticketing facility, TKITT also provides our valuable customers with document attestation. We help them attest their educational and non-educational documents, which include import and export certificates at the relevant offices including the Pakistani Foreign Office.
